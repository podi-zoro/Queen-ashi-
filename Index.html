Complete Tutorial Guide

Learn how to use QUEEN ASHI MD step by step

Step 1: Pair Your Device

Click "Pair Now" button and follow the instructions to connect your WhatsApp with the bot.

Step 2: Use Basic Commands

Start with simple commands: .menu, .help, .ping to test the bot response.

Step 3: Music & Media Features

Use .song [name] to download music, .video [url] for videos, .status to save WhatsApp status.

Step 4: Games & Fun

Try .game to see available games, .quiz for trivia, .meme for fun content.

Step 5: Group Features

Add bot to group and use .welcome on, .antilink on, .tagall for group management.
